# Ideas

Unstructured — raw ideas as they come up, not yet vetted or scheduled. When an idea is decided on, move it into [../docs/10_Roadmap.md](../docs/10_Roadmap.md) (if it's a planned feature) or [FutureFeatures.md](FutureFeatures.md) (if it's parked for later). When an idea is rejected, leave a one-line note here on why, so it doesn't get re-proposed without reason.
